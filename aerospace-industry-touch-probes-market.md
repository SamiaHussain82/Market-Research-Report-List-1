<p><p>The aerospace industry utilizes various touch probes for precision measurements and quality control. Contact type probes physically touch the surface for data collection, ensuring high accuracy. Electronic type probes use sensors to detect part interactions without physical contact, enhancing speed and reducing wear. Infrared type probes measure temperature and surface characteristics using infrared signals, providing non-intrusive data. Scanning type probes continuously collect data over a surface, allowing for detailed mapping. Other types may include specialized probes designed for unique aerospace applications.</p></p>
