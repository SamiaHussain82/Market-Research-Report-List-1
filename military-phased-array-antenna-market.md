<p><strong>The Military Phased Array Antenna Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>AESA</li><li>PESA</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The military phased array antenna market is primarily divided into two types: Active Electronically Scanned Arrays (AESA) and Passive Electronically Scanned Arrays (PESA). AESA antennas use individual transmit/receive modules to dynamically steer beams, offering superior performance in terms of agility, flexibility, and reliability for advanced radar systems. PESA antennas, on the other hand, employ a single powerful transmitter with multiple receiver elements to control beam direction, providing a more cost-effective option while maintaining effective tracking and surveillance capabilities.</p></p>
