<p><p>The Human Rabies Vaccine Market is divided into pre-exposure and post-exposure applications. Pre-exposure vaccines are administered to individuals at higher risk, such as animal handlers or travelers to endemic areas, providing immunity before potential exposure. In contrast, post-exposure vaccines are crucial for individuals bitten by potentially rabid animals, initiating treatment to prevent the onset of the disease. Both applications are integral to rabies prevention strategies, ensuring public health safety and reducing the risk of fatal outcomes from this viral infection.</p></p>
