<p><p>Platelet-rich Plasma (PRP) therapy is categorized into three main types: Pure PRP, which contains a high concentration of platelets and minimal white blood cells, making it suitable for aesthetic and orthopedic applications; Leucocyte-rich PRP, which includes higher white blood cell content, enhancing anti-inflammatory effects and used in various healing processes; and Leukocyte-rich Fibrin (L-PRF), a three-dimensional matrix of platelets and leukocytes, promoting tissue regeneration and commonly used in dental and surgical applications. Each type serves distinct therapeutic needs.</p></p>
