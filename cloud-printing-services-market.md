<p><p>Cloud printing services can be categorized into three market types: Public Cloud, Private Cloud, and Dual Cloud. Public Cloud utilizes shared resources over the internet, making printing accessible to multiple users at a lower cost. Private Cloud offers dedicated resources for individual organizations, enhancing security and control. Dual Cloud combines both models, allowing organizations to leverage the benefits of public services while maintaining a private environment for sensitive documents. Each type caters to different organizational needs and security requirements.</p></p>
