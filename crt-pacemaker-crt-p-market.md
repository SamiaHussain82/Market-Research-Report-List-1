<p><p>The CRT Pacemaker (CRT-P) market comprises three primary types: Extracorporeal Portable Pacemakers, which are external devices providing temporary pacing; Implanted Pacemakers, designed for long-term management by being surgically placed inside the body for continuous heart rhythm support; and Semi-Buried Pacemakers, which offer a compromise between external and fully implanted systems, partially embedded for easier access while providing longer-term pacing. Each type caters to different patient needs, focusing on effectiveness, comfort, and ease of use.</p></p>
