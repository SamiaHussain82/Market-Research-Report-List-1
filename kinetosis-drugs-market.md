Antihistamines, like meclizine and dimenhydrinate, alleviate symptoms by counteracting the effects of histamine and reducing nausea. The "others" category encompasses alternative treatments, including herbal remedies and newer drug formulations that target kinetosis by different mechanisms. Each type plays a critical role in providing relief from motion sickness.</p></p>
