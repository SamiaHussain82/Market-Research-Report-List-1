<p><ul><li>Total Parenteral Solution</li><li>Peripheral Parenteral Solution</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The intravenous (IV) solution market is primarily divided into two main types: Total Parenteral Nutrition (TPN) and Peripheral Parenteral Nutrition (PPN). TPN provides comprehensive nutritional support delivered directly into the bloodstream, ideal for patients with severe digestive issues or unable to consume food orally. In contrast, PPN offers partial nutritional supplementation through a peripheral vein, suitable for patients who can tolerate some oral intake but require additional nutrients for recovery. Both markets cater to specific patient needs.</p></p>
