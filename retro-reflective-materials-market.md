<p><p>Retro-reflective materials are designed to return light to its source, enhancing visibility in low-light conditions. The market consists primarily of two types: microprismatic materials and glass beads. </p><p>Microprismatic retro-reflective materials utilize tiny prisms to reflect light efficiently, providing superior visibility and durability, often used in traffic signage and safety apparel. Glass beads, on the other hand, consist of tiny spheres embedded in a layer, reflecting light through refraction, commonly found in road markings and reflective tapes. Both types enhance safety and awareness in various applications.</p></p>
