<p><p>The sealed tactile switches market is divided primarily into surface-mounting and through-hole mounting types. Surface-mounting switches are designed for modern circuit boards, allowing for a compact design and efficient use of space, making them popular in consumer electronics. In contrast, through-hole mounting switches are inserted into pre-drilled holes on the circuit board, offering robust physical stability, which is beneficial for high-stress applications. Both types cater to diverse industries, ensuring reliable performance in various electronic devices.</p></p>
