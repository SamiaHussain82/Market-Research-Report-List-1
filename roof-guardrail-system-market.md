<p><strong>The Roof Guardrail System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-penetrating Guardrail System</li><li>Fixed Guardrail System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The roof guardrail system market primarily features two types: non-penetrating guardrail systems and fixed guardrail systems. Non-penetrating guardrail systems are designed to be installed without making holes in the roof, ensuring roof integrity while providing safety. They use weighted bases to remain stable. In contrast, fixed guardrail systems are securely mounted to the roof structure, offering a permanent safety solution. Both types are essential for preventing falls and ensuring compliance with safety regulations in construction and maintenance environments.</p></p>
