<p><p>The skin resurfacing machine market is divided into two main types: ablative and non-ablative. Ablative devices remove the outer layers of skin, promoting healing and collagen production, thereby effectively addressing scars, wrinkles, and uneven skin texture. Non-ablative machines, on the other hand, target deeper skin layers without damaging the surface, utilizing methods like laser therapy to stimulate collagen growth and improve skin tone without significant downtime. Each type serves distinct cosmetic needs, catering to varying patient preferences and skin conditions.</p></p>
