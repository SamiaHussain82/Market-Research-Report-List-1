<p><p>The infrared night-vision scope market is categorized primarily into two types: single tube and double tube scopes. Single tube infrared night-vision scopes utilize a single optical path to provide a compact and lightweight design, making them suitable for various applications, including hunting and security. In contrast, double tube infrared night-vision scopes incorporate two separate optical paths, offering superior depth perception and enhanced clarity, ideal for more demanding scenarios, such as military operations and professional tactical use.</p></p>
