<p><ul><li>Quicklime</li><li>Slaked Lime</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ag-Lime market consists of various types, primarily focusing on Quicklime and Slaked Lime. Quicklime, or calcium oxide, is used in agriculture to enhance soil pH and fertility. It is produced by heating limestone, resulting in a highly reactive form of lime. Slaked Lime, or calcium hydroxide, is produced by adding water to Quicklime. It serves to neutralize acidic soil conditions and improves nutrient availability for crops. Both types play crucial roles in sustainable agricultural practices and soil management.</p></p>
