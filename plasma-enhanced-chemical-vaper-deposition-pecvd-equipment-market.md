<p><p>Plasma Enhanced Chemical Vapor Deposition (PECVD) equipment is a sophisticated technology used to deposit thin films on various substrates through chemical vapor deposition processes enhanced by plasma. PECVD is widely utilized in industries such as semiconductors, photovoltaics, microelectronics, and optics, where precision and film quality are paramount.</p><p>The PECVD equipment market is experiencing robust growth, driven by the increasing demand for advanced materials and nanotechnology applications.
