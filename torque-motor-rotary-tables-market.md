<p><p>Torque motor rotary tables are specialized devices used for precise positioning in various industrial applications. The market can be categorized into two main types: external rotation structure and internal rotation structure. External rotation structures feature a motor located outside the rotating axis, providing higher torque and stability, making them suitable for heavy loads. In contrast, internal rotation structures integrate the motor within the table, allowing for compact designs and enhanced precision in smaller applications, ideal for limited spaces and intricate operations.</p></p>
