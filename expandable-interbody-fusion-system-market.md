<p><p>The Expandable Interbody Fusion System market consists of three main types: Lumbar System, Thoraco-lumbar System, and Cervical System. The Lumbar System focuses on lower back surgeries, promoting stability and fusion in the lumbar region. Thoraco-lumbar System caters to both thoracic and lumbar spine issues, addressing mid to lower back challenges. The Cervical System targets the neck region, facilitating spinal fusion and alleviating nerve compression. Each system plays a vital role in enhancing surgical outcomes and patient recovery in spinal procedures.</p></p>
