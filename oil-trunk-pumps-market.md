<p><strong>The Oil-trunk Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tube Pump</li><li>Rod Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The oil-trunk pumps market primarily features two types: tube pumps and rod pumps. Tube pumps utilize a flexible tube to transport oil, providing a gentle and efficient method for fluid transfer, suitable for low-viscosity oils. In contrast, rod pumps employ a reciprocating motion driven by a rod system, ideal for lifting heavier oils from underground reservoirs. Both types cater to the diverse needs of the oil industry, ensuring effective extraction and transportation while adapting to varying operational conditions.</p></p>
