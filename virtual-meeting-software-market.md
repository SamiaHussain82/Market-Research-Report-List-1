<p><p>The virtual meeting software market is divided into two main types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based software is hosted on remote servers, allowing users to access the platform from anywhere with an internet connection, promoting scalability and flexibility. In contrast, on-premise software is installed locally on a user's hardware, offering greater control over data security and customization, but requiring significant IT resources for maintenance. Both types cater to varying organizational needs and preferences in the evolving digital communication landscape.</p></p>
