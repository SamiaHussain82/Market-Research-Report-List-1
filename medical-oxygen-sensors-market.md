<p><ul><li>Zirconia Oxygen Sensor</li><li>Optical Oxygen Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical oxygen sensors market primarily comprises two types: zirconia oxygen sensors and optical oxygen sensors. Zirconia oxygen sensors utilize zirconium dioxide to measure oxygen concentration based on electrical conductivity changes at high temperatures, offering accurate readings in critical care settings. In contrast, optical oxygen sensors employ luminescent materials that respond to oxygen presence, providing non-invasive and quick measurements. Both types are essential for patient monitoring in hospitals, ensuring appropriate oxygen levels during anesthesia, intensive care, and respiratory treatments.</p></p>
