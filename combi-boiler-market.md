<p><p>A combi boiler, or combination boiler, is a highly efficient heating system that provides both hot water and central heating from a single unit. Additionally, the rise in smart home systems is fostering the integration of smart combi boilers, offering users enhanced control and monitoring capabilities. These trends highlight a shift towards more sustainable and technologically advanced heating solutions in the residential sector, positioning combi boilers as a preferred choice for modern homeowners.</p></p>
