<p><ul><li>Wet Heat Bain Maries</li><li>Dry Heat Bain Maries</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial bain marie market is categorized into two main types: wet heat and dry heat bain maries. Wet heat bain maries utilize water to maintain food temperatures, ensuring moisture retention and preventing drying, making them ideal for sauces and delicate dishes. In contrast, dry heat bain maries use heated air or electric elements, providing a more controlled environment suited for solid foods. Both types serve essential roles in food service for keeping items warm and ready for serving in restaurants and catering environments.</p></p>
