<p><p>The photovoltaic connector market is divided into two main types: AC and DC connectors. AC photovoltaic connectors are designed for systems that convert solar energy into alternating current, facilitating grid connection and energy distribution. In contrast, DC photovoltaic connectors are used in direct current systems, typically linking solar panels to inverters. Both connector types are essential for ensuring safe and efficient energy transfer in solar installations, catering to various applications and requirements within the renewable energy sector.</p></p>
