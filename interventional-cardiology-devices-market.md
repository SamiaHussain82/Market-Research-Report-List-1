<p><p>The interventional cardiology devices market encompasses various categories that aid in treating cardiovascular disorders. Angioplasty balloons help widen narrowed arteries, while angioplasty stents provide structural support to keep vessels open. Catheters are used for diagnosis and treatment, facilitating the delivery of therapies. Plaque modification devices target atherosclerotic plaques to restore blood flow, and hemodynamic flow alteration devices manage blood circulation dynamics. Together, these tools play a crucial role in improving heart health and patient outcomes.</p></p>
