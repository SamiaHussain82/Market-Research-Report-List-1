<p><p>C-Band satellite transponders are categorized into high frequency, intermediate frequency, and low frequency, each serving distinct roles in satellite communications. High frequency transponders, operating above 5.850 GHz, provide high data rates suitable for internet and video services. Intermediate frequency transponders, typically ranging from 5.850 to 6.425 GHz, offer a balanced performance for various telecommunications applications. Low frequency transponders, below 5.850 GHz, are often used for wider coverage and penetrating dense environments, ensuring reliable connectivity for diverse customers.</p></p>
