<p><p>The Paclitaxel Coated PTCA (Percutaneous Transluminal Coronary Angioplasty) Balloon Catheter is a medical device used in the treatment of coronary artery diseases. This innovative approach enhances the efficacy of angioplasty by minimizing complications and improving long-term patient outcomes.</p><p>The market for Paclitaxel Coated PTCA Balloon Catheters is witnessing significant growth, driven by an increasing prevalence of cardiovascular diseases and a rising aging population.
