<p><p>The Passenger Vehicle Thermostats market comprises two main types: insert thermostats and housing thermostats. Insert thermostats are designed to be directly fitted into the engine cooling system, regulating the coolant flow based on temperature, thus optimizing engine performance and preventing overheating. Housing thermostats, on the other hand, come integrated within a housing unit that includes the thermostat and other components, simplifying installation and maintenance. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring efficient vehicle operation and longevity.</p></p>
