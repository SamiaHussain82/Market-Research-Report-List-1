<p><p>The Flight Propulsion System market is divided into two primary types: air-breathing engines and non-air-breathing engines. Air-breathing engines, such as turbojets and turbofans, utilize atmospheric oxygen for combustion, making them suitable for subsonic and supersonic flight. Non-air-breathing engines, like rocket engines, carry both fuel and oxidizer, enabling operation in space where oxygen is absent. These systems are crucial for various applications, including commercial aviation, military operations, and space exploration, highlighting their importance in modern aerospace technology.</p></p>
