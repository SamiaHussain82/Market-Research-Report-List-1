<p><strong>The Portable Diagnostic Ultrasound Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>A-mode</li><li>B-mode</li><li>C-mode</li><li>F-mode</li><li>M-mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Portable Diagnostic Ultrasound Machines market includes various modes that cater to different imaging needs. A-mode (Amplitude mode) provides a single-dimensional view, useful in ophthalmology. B-mode (Brightness mode) offers a two-dimensional grayscale image, widely used for diagnostics. C-mode (Contrast mode) enhances tissue contrast for better visualization. F-mode (Flow mode) assesses blood flow, particularly in vascular studies. M-mode (Motion mode) captures the motion of structures over time, essential for cardiac analysis. Each mode serves specific clinical applications, driving market diversity.</p></p>
