<p><p>The roughness and contour measuring machine market is divided into two primary types: contact and non-contact machines. Contact roughness and contour measuring machines utilize tactile sensors to physically touch the surface for precise measurements, making them suitable for various materials. In contrast, non-contact measuring machines employ optical or laser technology to assess surface characteristics without physical contact, ideal for delicate surfaces or when minimal disturbance is required. Both types serve essential roles in quality control and surface analysis across multiple industries.</p></p>
