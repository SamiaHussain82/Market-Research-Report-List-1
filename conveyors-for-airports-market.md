<p><p>The conveyors for airports market primarily includes belt conveyors and roller conveyors, each serving distinct functions. Belt conveyors are ideal for moving baggage efficiently over long distances, featuring a continuous loop system that minimizes the risk of damage to luggage. Roller conveyors, on the other hand, consist of a series of rollers that facilitate the easy movement of bags, especially in sorting areas. Both types enhance operational efficiency, streamline baggage handling, and improve the overall passenger experience at airports.</p></p>
