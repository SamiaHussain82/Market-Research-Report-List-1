<p><p>The sachet packaging market is categorized into four key types: paper, plastic, cellulosic, and aluminum foil. Paper sachets are eco-friendly and biodegradable, suitable for dry products. Plastic sachets, versatile and cost-effective, are widely used for liquids and powders. Cellulosic sachets, made from renewable resources, offer excellent barrier properties while being biodegradable. Aluminum foil sachets provide superior protection against moisture, light, and oxygen, making them ideal for preserving the freshness of sensitive products like food and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
