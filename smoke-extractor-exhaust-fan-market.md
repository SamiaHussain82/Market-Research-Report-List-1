<p><p>The smoke extractor exhaust fan market consists of various types, primarily categorized into centrifugal and axial fans, along with other specialized designs. Centrifugal fans utilize a rotating impeller to generate high air pressure and efficiently remove smoke and fumes from enclosed spaces. Axial fans, on the other hand, move air parallel to the fan's axis, providing high-volume airflow suitable for large areas. Other types may include specialized or hybrid designs tailored for specific applications, enhancing smoke control and ventilation in various environments.</p></p>
