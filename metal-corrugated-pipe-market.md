<p><p>The metal corrugated pipe market consists of two primary types: single wall corrugated and double wall corrugated pipes. Single wall corrugated pipes feature a single layer, providing flexibility and lightweight properties, ideal for drainage and ventilation applications. In contrast, double wall corrugated pipes consist of two layers, offering enhanced strength, durability, and resistance to external pressures while maintaining a lightweight design. Both types are utilized in various sectors, including construction, infrastructure, and industrial applications, owing to their performance and reliability.</p></p>
